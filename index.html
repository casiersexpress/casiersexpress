Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend reaching out at least 2–3 weeks before your event. That said, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests — submit a quote and we'll confirm availability within 48 hours.

Do you deliver and set up the lockers?

Yes! Delivery, installation, and pickup are all included in your quote. Our team handles everything so you can focus on running your event.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve events in Quebec and Ontario. Reach out for events outside these regions — we may still be able to help depending on logistics.

Are the lockers weatherproof?

Our lockers are durable and built for outdoor use. For extended outdoor exposure or harsh weather conditions, we can include a tent covering with your rental.

Can I brand the lockers with my event's logo?

Absolutely. We offer vinyl wrap branding so your lockers match your event's identity. Just indicate it in your quote request and we'll include it.

Can I charge attendees to use the lockers?

Yes — many organizers charge attendees per locker to offset the rental cost. You set your own pricing and handle collection. We handle the equipment.
